About Us

BIRYANI USTAD

At Biryani Ustad, we elevate biryani to a fine art, blending tradition and taste in every aromatic plate. With recipes passed down through generations and perfected over time, our biryanis are crafted using only the freshest ingredients and authentic spices. Whether you’re craving the timeless Hyderabadi Biryani, a royal Mughlai Biryani, or a flavorful Vegetarian Biryani, we promise a culinary experience like no other.

Our Signature Biryani

Every dish at Biryani Ustad is an ode to the rich history of biryani. Our range includes succulent chicken, mutton, seafood, and vegetarian options, each prepared with fragrant basmati rice, slow-cooked meats, and a medley of spices that create an irresistible symphony of flavors.

Why Dine at Biryani Ustad?

  • Authenticity: We use traditional cooking methods and time-honored recipes to bring the true essence of biryani to your plate.
  • Master Chefs: Our expert chefs are the true Ustads (masters) in biryani-making, ensuring each dish is a masterpiece.
  • Cozy Ambiance: Dine in a setting that offers comfort with a touch of cultural flair, perfect for family gatherings or intimate meals.
  • Quick Delivery: Enjoy the taste of Biryani Ustad at home with our fast and reliable delivery service.

Our Menu

  • Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani: Tender chicken slow-cooked in layers of spiced basmati rice.
  • Mutton Biryani: Juicy mutton blended with aromatic herbs and spices, creating a rich and flavorful dish.
  • Seafood Biryani: A delicious mix of prawns and fish, infused with spices and cooked to perfection.
  • Vegetarian Biryani: A feast for the veggie lovers, packed with farm-fresh vegetables and flavorful rice.
  • Ustads Special Platters: Ideal for sharing or enjoying on your own, these platters are curated to showcase the best of our menu.

Catering & Events

Make your next event unforgettable with Biryani Ustads catering services. Whether its a wedding, birthday party, or corporate event, our team ensures that every guest is treated to a feast of mouthwatering biryani and more.

About Us

Biryani Ustad is a gateway to experiencing the rich culinary heritage of Hyderabad, famed for its distinctive biryani. Stepping into Biryani Ustad, patrons are immediately greeted by the aroma of fragrant basmati rice, saffron, and spices that define the restaurant's signature dish.

Biryani Ustad is not just a place to dine; its an experience in itself. The restaurant takes pride in staying true to authentic recipes passed down through generations, ensuring that every bite offers a taste of history and culture. Whether you're a local or a traveler, the irresistible aroma and flavors of the biryani make Biryani Ustad a must-visit destination for food lovers seeking an unforgettable culinary journey.
